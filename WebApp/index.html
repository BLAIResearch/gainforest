The smart contract has three use cases: donations, caretakers and the oracle.

Donations: a donator makes a monetary donation into the smart contract. The donator does not decide to which patch of forest the money is going and does not decide to whom the money is going or in what amounts. All the donations are mixed into a $donationsJar

Caretakers: you take responsability of ensuring a certain patch of the amazon rainforest is taken care of against deforestation. We have divided the forest into manageable small squares. Caretakers select a square and then stake a voluntary amount of money. After our iteration time (1/3/6 months) if the square is still a standing forest then the smart contract returns the original stake to the caregiver pus a reward on top of it. If the square is deforested, the caregiver forfeits the stake.
